Welcome

please meet my animal friends

Mammals

Caleb the Fox

I met Caleb in the forest in Minnesota. We became fast friends. He loves to run around and play. He enjoys practical jokes, so be aware if you run into him. If you scratch behind his ears, he will be your friend forever.


Please click here for Caleb's webpage!

Sheila the Wolf

I met Shiela in Northern Minnesota. It took me awhile to get to know her because of her timid nature. When she does care about someone, she is very brave and loyal. She is currently searching for a pack.

Steve the Longhorn

I met steve in Texas while he was trying to cross the road. I got out of my car and helped him find the chicken he was following. He is quite determined and single-minded, but he also knows how to relax. He loves to have his horns stroked.

Melissa the Bat

I found Melissa hanging around my apartment in Massachusetts. It was daytime, so I waited until she woke up at night to disturb her. She eats all the stray bugs, so I let her stay around. She sure can chat up a storm!

Birds

Lily the African Grey

Lily is a stray. I rescued her from a poacher down the street in Massachusetts. I invied her to stay with me, but she likes to fly free. She is extremely intelligent and can communicate in English extremely effectively. She even uses her speaking skills to convince people to give her what she wants. Unlike most parrots, she doesn't mind the cold.

Raul the Galah

Raul started off as my sister's friend. He landed on her head one day and refused to leave. He talks almost nonstop and says the most absurd things. He will do anything to get a laugh out of you. He loves all things pink and will only consider a pink mate. He currently lives with my parents in Florida.

Archibald the Chicken

Archibald is the chicken who crossed the road. He had no real aim except to go where the wind took him. He is a dreamer always wishing to reach new heights. Unfortunately, his wings do not cary him too far. He ignores the ladies in favor of his mammalian companions. He lives in Texas with his friend Steve.

Janet the Red Tailed Hawk

I met Janet amongst the trees in Kentucky. She was searching for prey but found me instead. She loves to play games such as hide and seek. She plays "chase" with small mammals, which she always wins. She is proud of her feathers and not afraid to show off.

Dragons

Agnus the Green Scaled Dragon

Agnus is the first dragon I ever met. I ran into her deep in the forest in Minnesota. I almost did not see her at first because her scales blend in with the leaves. She is a docile dragon, preferring to live in harmony with the forest animals. She allowed me to lean against her while I read.

Hilda the Brown Scaled Dragon

I first spotted Hilda scouring the blue hills of Kentucky. At first I thought she was hunting, but then I realized she was looking for someone to play with! She allowed me to ride on her back and took me on a glroious adventure. Then we enjoyed a feast, which she produced herself.

Peter the Red Scaled Dragon

Peter is a fierce Texas dragon I met on my morning walk. He patrols the land, defending it against any intruders. He sometimes takes me hunting and teaches me his ways. He is a good friend, but if you cross him, beware his fiery breath!

Arthur the Grey Scaled Dragon

I met Arthur recently in Massachusetts, and he is already my best friend. He enjoys intellectual pursuits versus physical ones. He never stops asking questions or posing ideas. He and Lily have a lot to talk about. The two of us enjoy the skies and often fly to the tops of mountains together while discussing philosophy. If any dragon is going to solve the world's problems, it is going to be him!
